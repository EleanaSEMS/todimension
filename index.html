Hello! Welcome to my website, ToDimension.

Since this website is still in beta, I'd like to explain a few things I wasn't exactly able to fix so easily, so you'll have to bear with me.

First, the stickers. When you place them in the notes, they're a little hard to rotate. I suggest you aim for just at the top of the sticker (Especially if you're on a computer) and you'll see it grab. That's when you rotate.

Again for the stickers, when you open the sticker book in the notes, you'll see this "Unlock background" feature. This allows you to move stickers that you have put behind the text in your notebook. To be able to add text to your notebook again, you simply lock the background.

In the notes, the broom emoji beside the highlight just means it removes the highlighter on your text.

Only saves per browser. I haven't added a sign in feature yet, but soon I will. For now, your progress only saves to your browser. It won't save anywhere else. Please let me know if you have any problems with this.

DO NOT FORGET to save your notes! If you exit without saving, well.. it won't save.

For adding files for your character sprites, voice lines, and themes. When you reload, it will go back to "No file chosen". This doesn't mean it did not save (unless you didn't save it). You can test it by opening your notes or the fact your theme background is still there once you reload. It will still be there.

Let me know if you have suggestions or problems you run into. Have fun!
